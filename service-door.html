Door to Door

Complete pickup-to-delivery service — we come to you, every step of the way

About This Service

Our Door-to-Door (TTD-Service) service is the most convenient way to send shipments across India. We pick up your parcel directly from your home, office, or warehouse and deliver it right to the recipient's doorstep — no trips needed, no standing in queues.

Whether it's a birthday gift to a family member in another city, an e-commerce return, or regular business dispatch — our riders come to you at your scheduled time.

Key Features

  • Scheduled pickup from your address — home, office, or warehouse
  • Delivery directly to recipient's doorstep, anywhere in India
  • Packaging assistance available at pickup
  • Live tracking from pickup to delivery
  • Multiple weight slabs from 500g to 10 kg
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) option available
