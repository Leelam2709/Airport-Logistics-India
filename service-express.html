Express Delivery

Same-day and next-day delivery for urgent shipments across India

About This Service

When time matters most, Express Delivery is your answer. We offer same-day delivery within city limits and next-day delivery to major metros across India. Our dedicated express riders and priority sorting ensure your parcel moves ahead of regular queue at every step.

Perfect for last-minute gifts, urgent business documents, legal papers, medical samples, and e-commerce orders where delivery speed is critical.

Key Features

  • Same-day delivery within city — book before 11:00 AM
  • Next-day delivery to 50+ metro and tier-1 cities
  • Dedicated express riders — no mixing with regular courier
  • Priority sorting at hub — your parcel moves first
  • Live GPS tracking every 30 minutes
  • Available 7 days/week excluding holidays (Sunday)
