International Courier

Send to 220+ countries with customs clearance and real-time tracking

About This Service

Our International Courier service connects India to 220+ countries worldwide. We handle customs documentation, export clearances, and partner with global carriers like FedEx, DHL, and UPS to ensure your parcel reaches anywhere in the world safely and on time.

From personal gifts to commercial samples and business documents — we make international shipping as simple as domestic delivery.

Key Features

  • Delivery to 220+ countries and territories worldwide
  • Customs documentation and export clearance support
  • Partnership with FedEx, DHL, UPS, Aramex
  • End-to-end tracking with international updates
  • Prohibited items guidance and compliance check
  • Insurance available for all international shipments

How It Works

1

Book & Submit Documents

Book online and submit commercial invoice and packing list for customs.

2

Export Customs Clearance

Our team processes your export declaration at origin airport.

3

International Flight Dispatch

Shipment departs India on partner airline with live tracking.

4

Import Clearance & Delivery

Clears customs at destination and delivered to recipient's door.
