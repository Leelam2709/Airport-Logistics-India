About Us

Moving India Forward

Airport Logistics India was founded with one mission — to make air logistics accessible, transparent, and reliable for every business in India.

Our Story

From a Single Office to a National Network

Airport Logistics India began in 2018 with a single operational hub at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, Mumbai. Founded by a team of logistics veterans and technology professionals, the company was built to address a fundamental problem — small and medium businesses in India had no access to affordable, trackable air courier services.

Today, we operate across 12 major airports, 120+ cities, and serve over 10,000 businesses ranging from solo entrepreneurs to large enterprises. Our MERN-powered platform connects customers directly to our logistics network, eliminating middlemen and giving complete shipment visibility in real time.

We are proud to be an Indian company serving Indian businesses — and connecting them to the world.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2018

Company Founded

Airport Logistics India incorporated in Mumbai. First operational hub launched at CSIA Terminal 2.

2019

Domestic Network Expanded

Reached 40 cities across India. Launched the web-based shipment tracking system v1.0.

2021

International Launch

Launched international freight services to UAE, UK, USA, and Singapore. Partnered with 20+ global carriers.

2023

Technology Upgrade – MERN Platform

Rebuilt the entire logistics platform using MERN stack. Introduced real-time tracking, automated customs docs, and the business analytics dashboard.

2025

10,000 Business Clients Milestone

Crossed 50,000 shipments delivered. Operating across 12 airport hubs and 120+ cities. Expanding to 5 new countries.
