Cargo & Freight

Heavy cargo and bulk freight solutions via air, road, and rail

About This Service

Our Cargo & Freight service is built for businesses that move large volumes. Whether it's machinery, industrial goods, raw materials, or commercial consignments — we provide end-to-end freight management across air, road, and rail networks.

We operate from all major Indian airports and have dedicated freight terminals for efficient loading, unloading, and customs handling of bulk cargo.

Freight Types

  • Air Freight — Priority air cargo for time-sensitive bulk shipments
  • Road Freight — Full truck load (FTL) and part truck load (PTL) options
  • Rail Freight — Cost-effective rail cargo for long-distance heavy loads
  • Multi-Modal — Combination of air + road + rail for optimal routing
  • Project Cargo — Specialized handling for oversized or sensitive equipment
