Airport - To - Airport

Fastest cargo transfer between India's major airports with priority handling

About This Service

Airport-to-Airport is our flagship express service designed for businesses and individuals who need the fastest possible transit times. Your cargo is collected at the origin airport, loaded onto the next available flight, and made available for pickup at the destination airport — all within hours.

Ideal for urgent documents, high-value goods, spare parts, medical supplies, and time-sensitive commercial cargo that cannot wait for regular courier timelines.

Key Features

  • Direct airport-to-airport transfer — no intermediate stops
  • Same-day dispatch on bookings before 12:00 PM
  • Priority loading on next available flight
  • Accepts documents, parcels, and cargo up to 500 kg
  • Insurance coverage available for all shipments
  • Dedicated customer support 24/7

How It Works

1

Book Your Shipment

Book online or visit our airport counter. Provide shipment details, weight, and destination airport.

2

Drop at Airport Counter

Bring your parcel to our ALI counter at the departure airport. We handle documentation and labeling.

3

Flight Dispatch

Your cargo is loaded on the next available flight to the destination city with priority boarding.

4

Collect at Destination

Recipient collects the shipment at the destination airport counter within 2 hours of flight landing.
